I recommend that each student has at least one session with me before committing to half a term of regular lessons, so we can make sure it's a good fit for everyone.

For longer sessions, you get significantly more value — we're able to cover more ground, practise exam technique, and really build momentum. If you're looking for your child to make substantial progress, a 1.5 or 2-hour session each week is the most effective option.
